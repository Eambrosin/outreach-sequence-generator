## Overview

AI Outreach Intelligence Platform is a Business Development and Partnerships application designed to help commercial teams prioritize follow-up opportunities, assess revenue risk, generate account intelligence and create multi-step outreach sequences.

The platform combines commercial reasoning, opportunity prioritization and AI-assisted outreach generation into a single workflow.

---

## Business Problem

Many opportunities stall after a proposal, quotation, FCO or commercial offer is sent.

Most teams:

- Lack a structured follow-up process
- Prioritize accounts inconsistently
- Fail to identify revenue at risk
- Use the same outreach approach across markets
- Miss opportunities due to poor timing

This platform helps commercial teams identify where to focus and how to engage.

---

## Key Features

### Executive Outreach Dashboard

- Pipeline visibility
- Revenue at risk
- Follow-up urgency
- Channel strategy

### Follow-Up Prioritization Engine

Ranks opportunities using:

- Deal value
- Proposal age
- Commercial urgency

### Business Development Intelligence Layer

Generates:

- Recommended motion
- Expansion potential
- Partnership potential
- Risk assessment
- Stakeholder recommendations

### AI Outreach Intelligence

Creates:

- Account Summary
- Commercial Opportunity
- Expansion Hypothesis
- Partnership Hypothesis
- Recommended Outreach Strategy
- Risk Assessment

### Multi-Step Outreach Sequences

- Day 1 Follow-up
- Day 3 Value Add
- Day 7 Re-engagement
- Day 14 Final Touch
